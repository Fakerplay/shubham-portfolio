# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Startup founders, brand leaders, creative directors, and hiring teams evaluating Shubham Shinde's ability to shape brand identities, digital experiences, motion-led visuals, and launch work. Visitors need to understand the point of view quickly, inspect credible project evidence, and contact Shubham with confidence.

## Product Purpose

Present a distinctive personal portfolio that turns Shubham's taste, range, and project outcomes into qualified project conversations. Success means the work is memorable, case studies are discoverable, the experience performs reliably across devices, and visitors can move from introduction to evidence to contact without friction.

## Brand Personality

Tactile, editorial, and playful. The site should feel authored and materially crafted, with confident interaction details and concise language.

## Anti-references

Generic AI-generated agency templates, interchangeable SaaS landing pages, decorative eyebrow labels, inflated marketing prose, repeated explanatory descriptions, anonymous card grids, and redesigns that erase the existing tactile portfolio identity.

## Design Principles

1. Show the work before explaining it.
2. Every visible word must contribute identity, evidence, or action.
3. Motion is personality, never a prerequisite for access to content.
4. Preserve the site's authored tactile character across every viewport.
5. Technical quality should make the visual experience feel effortless.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain semantic headings and landmarks, descriptive image alternatives, keyboard-accessible controls, clear focus behavior, reduced-motion support, readable contrast across themes, touch-safe targets, and content that remains visible when animation or WebGL is unavailable.
